Welcome to PodCats's Website! Here at our website, you will discover about the company's characteristics, goals, and plans. Starting with our goals, the company aims to produce the following:

  • Our content aims to produce a productive discussion between the speakers regarding the effects of Blended Learning to students in terms of their academic performance.
  • We aim to successfully provide an accurate list of effects of Blended Learning using facts and evidence.
  • We aim to follow the timeline in the proper time without delaying or advancing.
  • We aim for the podcast to be entertaining as much as it is informational.

As you can see above it's an image of a mind map that the company has used to determine the topic for their 1st podcast streaming. After the members brainstormed ideas through the mind map, the team was able to connect and highlighted different possible discussions for the stream. Varying from debate, discussions, to sharing.
